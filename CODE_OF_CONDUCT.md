# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance,
race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Sexualized language or imagery, and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of
acceptable behavior. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code,
issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the maintainers through GitHub. All complaints will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly.
